

## BOSON White Water — Media Enhancement Plan

This plan updates the BOSON White Water landing page with relevant water treatment imagery and video across three key sections.

### 1. Hero Section — Replace Background Video
- Remove the current waterfall/nature video
- Replace with a stock video of a **water treatment plant in operation** (e.g., filtration systems, treated water flowing, industrial RO/membrane plants) sourced from Pexels or similar free stock
- Keep the existing dark overlay gradient and all text/CTAs unchanged

### 2. How It Works Section — Water Treatment Step Images
- Replace the current generic Unsplash images for each of the 11 treatment stages with more relevant water treatment images:
  - **Filtration stages** → images of industrial water filters, membrane housings, filtration equipment
  - **Chemical dosing stages** (pH correction, antiscalant) → lab/chemical dosing equipment images
  - **Odour reduction** → activated carbon filter images
  - **XFR Membrane** → RO/membrane technology close-ups
  - **Disinfection** → UV treatment systems
- Each image will be sourced from free stock libraries and be contextually relevant to that specific treatment step

### 3. Benefits Section — Background Images on Cards
- Add subtle, semi-transparent **background images** to each of the 6 benefit cards:
  - **Zero CapEx** → industrial equipment/plant image
  - **65% Chemical Reduction** → chemistry/lab image
  - **Real-Time Monitoring** → IoT dashboard/sensor image
  - **Extended Asset Life** → clean cooling tower image
  - **ZLD Compliant** → water recycling/STP image
  - **Reduced Blowdown** → water flow/conservation image
- Images will be overlaid with the existing card background color so text remains readable
- Icons will remain on top of the imagery
